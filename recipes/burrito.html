Burrito

Description

A burrito is a Mexican dish that consists of a flour tortilla wrapped around a filling of meat, beans, cheese, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • tortilla
  • rice
  • black beans
  • salsa
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and black beans.
  2. Add the salsa and cheese to the rice and black beans.
  3. Wrap the rice and black beans in a tortilla.
